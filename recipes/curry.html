what you'll need

  • 2 cans chickpeas
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • spinach
  • rice for serving
