Classic Pizza Dough

lets do this

what you'll need

  • 4 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

how to proceed

  1. mix water, yeast, and sugar
  2. wait 5 minutes for yeast to bloom
  3. mix in flour, oil, and salt
  4. knead for 10 minutes
  5. let rise for 1 hour
  6. punch down and divide
  7. shape into pizza bases
  8. add toppings and bake
